VANILLA101

VANILLA DXMP; WITH A DIGITAL FACELIFT

VANILLA101 is a small gametype modification to Deus Ex Multiplayer, designed to play exactly the way DXMP was intended, while adding QOL features, commands and other extras.

VANILLA101 touches nothing regarding weapon, player or aug functionality, everything works the same as it did day one of the DXMP release, while adding extra ease of access commands for both players and server admins.
