NAOMI

Japanese DXMP Patch

NAOMI (or NAOMIdx or NAOMIdxmp or dxNAOMI or NAOdxMI) is an enviromental modification to Deus Ex Multiplayer, designed to redesign and reformat the weapons of the original DXMP to fit that of the Japanese region-locked release of Deus Ex, and subsequent to such, the Japanese DXMP; which had different weapon balancing to the DXMP released to the rest of the world.

NAOMI is a fork of ANNA, a DXMP gametype mod which fixes several gameplay bugs.

Browse the above links for more information, notes, and downloads.

NAOMI is not yet complete and still in development (current build inDev), the game should play normally despite this, but expect a few uncommon bugs either way.
